My Pic Collage Creation

1.Why did I use the photo to make the creation ?

Today, I want to introduce the photo and briefly explain how adorable the two girls are. Both of them are my nieces. By the way, the right side of the photo is a two-year-old little girl who is younger than the other. I use the photo to design the creation because of their facial expression representing opposite feelings. The right-side girl seems not to realize how her sister cried dramatically; consequently, she tried to take care of her.However, her sister cried much more loudly and she explained why she got a sorrowful feeling. At the meantime, I got the timing point and took a picture of them.The event is interesting for me and this is the reason why I choose the photo to make my creation.

2.How did I design the creation ?

I design the image by using Photoshop to remove the background and through PicCollage to edit it.
